MEZZ

Really Good House DJ

MEZZ is a dubstep artist, and is very versatile within the genre. He has experience mixing for a wide variety of different events and shows, but produces and performs bass music. MEZZ has established a presence in his local scene by helping curate events and putting out music with other local artists. He has developed his sound over the years by drawing inspiration from artists like MeSo, VCTRE, XOTIX, and his local scene. He continues to show his ability to produce, perform, and achieve new heights within the space.
