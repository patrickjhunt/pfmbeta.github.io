Really Good House DJ

MEZZ is a dubstep artist, and is very versatile within the genre. He has experience mixing for a wide variety of different events and shows, but produces and performs bass music. MEZZ has established a presence in his local scene by helping curate events and putting out music with other local artists. He continues to show his ability to produce, perform, and achieve new heights within the space.
